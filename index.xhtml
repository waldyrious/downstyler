Unstyle

A tool to fight CSS bloat.

Learn more in the README.

Usage

unstyle!

Just drag the link above to your browser's bookmarks toolbar.
You can then use it to instantly unstyle any page on the web.*
(Hint: click the link to try it on this page!)

✻

Alternative bookmarklets are available for a sans-serif mode,
as well as a "readability mode" that emulates a paper sheet:

unstyle+sans unstyle+read unstyle+read+sans

✻

Unstyle can also be used as a regular stylesheet.
Download unstyle.css, or include the following line in your HTML: 

<link rel="stylesheet"
href="http://rawgit.com/waldyrious/unstyle/master/unstyle.css" />
